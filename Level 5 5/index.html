Do not love half lovers. Do not entertain half friends.
Do not indulge in works of the half talented. Do not live half a life.
and do not die a half death. If you choose silence, then be silent
When you speak, do so until you are finished. Do not silence yourself to say something.
And do not speak to be silent. If you accept, then express it bluntly.
Do not mask it. If you refuse then be clear about it.
for an ambiguous refusal is but a weak acceptance. The half is a mere moment of inability.
but you are able for you are not half a being. You are a whole that exists to live a life not half a life.
